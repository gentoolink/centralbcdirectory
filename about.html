About Ask Central BC

Accurate local business information, powered by community

The Problem

We've all been there: you're looking for a specific service in your area, and you can't find accurate information. Is that restaurant still open? Did that shop move? Is there a new mechanic in town?

Local business directories get outdated fast. Google listings are incomplete. Word-of-mouth is unreliable. The result? People waste time searching, calling closed numbers, and getting frustrated.

The Solution

Ask Central BC is a simple AI chat interface that answers questions about local businesses — but with a human difference. Every business in our directory is verified by us, not crowdsourced. Hours are current. Status is accurate. Closed businesses don't appear.

You ask a question. You get a real answer. No ads, no outdated data, no guessing.

Who's Behind This

Ask Central BC is operated by Gentoolink Web Services Inc., a web design and development company based in Vanderhoof.

Ken McGonigal, sole owner, is a web designer and developer who spent 12 years as a police officer in South BC before transitioning to tech in 2020. He moved to Vanderhoof in 2022 and created Ask Central BC to solve a real community problem: the frustration of inaccurate local business information.

How We Verify

Accuracy is everything. When a business signs up, we verify the data through:

  • Contacting the business directly (phone call or site visit)
  • Confirming current hours and services
  • Checking for recent changes or closures
  • Removing outdated or inactive listings

Listings are added as soon as verification is complete — usually within a few days. Sponsored Partners get priority updates.

The Expansion Plan

We're starting in Vanderhoof because it's the geographic heart of Central BC. If this works, we're expanding along Highway 97 — Prince George, Kamloops, and the communities in between.

The goal: a verified, AI-powered directory that actually stays current, one community at a time.
